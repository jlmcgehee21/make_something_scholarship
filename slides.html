* My name is Jeff McGehee, I graduated from LCHS in 2007 * And I'm here to tell you that you should make something

Human or Computer?

* Part of my job is to build software that utilizes cutting edge artificial intelligence techniques. * You've probably heard of artificial intelligence before, or more likely seen it in movies. * But to give you an idea of *just one* of the things it's capable of, we'll play a short game.
* You can see that computers are remarkably good at creating artwork -- a very human skill

Why Should I Care?

* Sounds neat, but why should I care? * What does this have to do with me?

Within the next 20 years, AI will replace many unskilled workers.

* This will be the prime of your career. * If you are unskilled, you may not have a job.

You have to learn skills that computers can't...

and you can do this by making things.

I'm not convinced, that sounds like science fiction...

* You might say "I'm not convinced, that sounds like science fiction..." * That's ok. Part of being an intelligent person means that I can see things from the point of view of others. * I'll talk about something else that may motivate you later.

To make something you need to know:

  • How to use tools.
  • How to solve complex problems.
* These are two things that computers are not great at doing on their own yet. * When you make something, you have to understand everything about what you have made. * Let's explore what it takes to make some things.

What does it take to make a...

Birdhouse

* Tools/Problems * Research the type of bird * Geometry * Tools * Wood Finishing

What does it take to make a...

Cake

* Tools/Problems * Knowledge of ingredients * Volume/Weight measurement * How to work a mixer * How to work an oven * Extensive knowledge about consistency of batter/icing

What does it take to make an...

Internal Combustion Engine

* Tools/Problems * Advanced knowledge of thermodynamics * Advanced knowledge of fluid dynamics * Advanced knowledge of kinematics * Advanced knowledge of material science * Advanced knowledge of control systems * Advanced knowledge of metal machining/fabrication

What does it take to make a...

Website

* Tools/Problems * Advanced knowledge of (at least one) programming language * Advanced knowledge of a database system * Advanced knowledge of how the internet works * Advanced knowledge of software design processes * Advanced knowledge of whatever you're making the website for

Now let's talk about

money.

* Now that we've done those exercises... * Remember how I said I had another thing that might motivate you to make things? * People who can make things, can use that skill to make money * The more complex the thing, the more money you will make. * Think about how the salary of a carpenter or baker might compare with the salary of an Engineer or a Softwar Developer

* Graduating college is important -- but which field you choose is also important. * Because some fields give you the skills you need to make things that are more complicated.
